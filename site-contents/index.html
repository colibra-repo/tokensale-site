Making InsuranceTrustworthy Again

Colibra is building the first client centric insurance company by democratising the claim handling process.

Watch video

Insurance needs our help

Over the years, insurance companies have become increasingly centralized, complex and profit-driven. Transparency about their solvency and claim handling is practically missing. Conflict of interest is ubiquitous, which means it's profitable for insurers to delay and reject their clients' claims. This makes fewer and fewer people use insurance to mitigate the risks to their health, business and property.

The insurance industry is on the brink of technology-driven change. The centralized claims handling process lacks transparency and fairness. The Morgan Stanley/BCG,Read Analysis

IBM validated that at least 58% show distrust in insurance industry. Issues at focus are Personal Experience and Transparancy of Price and Terms&Conditions. IBM Global Services Institute, Read Analysis

A survey done by PwC in the UK found that there is 73% distrust in insurance providers. PWC United Kingdom,View Survey Results

Problem

The Democratic Insurance Company

Combining the power of blockchain, smart contracts, stablecoins, tokens, oracles, AI and gamified crowdsourced claim decisions, Colibra is building a new type of insurance company that can change the insurance industry for good.

Democratic Claim Handling

Colibra can’t reject or delay claim payments like traditional insurers because the claim handling process is outsourced to independent jurors on the blockchain.

Instant Payments

When a claim is approved by the community, a smart contract instantly triggers a payment to the client. 100% autonomously from the insurer.
