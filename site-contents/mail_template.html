3. First Product - Colibra FairFlight

Almost every traveller has had delayed flights but only a tiny fraction of them have received a compensation for that thanks to the EU 261 directive. This is due to many reasons some of which:

- Delayed time should be 3+ hours (or Cancelled)
- Delay should not be due to weather conditions
- Most airlines make it notoriously hard to receive your compensation (go to court or wait a few months)

Colibra's first product FairFlight gets you an instant compensation if your flight gets delayed for ANY reason with more than 1 hours. All you have to do is use our mobile boarding pass wallet.

And guess what...

You neither have to pay anything upfront nor have to pay post compensation. Awesome, isn't it!

Here's how the magic works:

Colibra's FairFlight is built on the EU261 directive which obligates airlines to pay a compensation for flights which are cancelled or delayed by more than 3 hours by fault of the airline. The odds of such an event happening is 200 to 1.

When such an event happens to someone using our zero upfront cost service, we take the compensation and instantly redistribute it to all the other flyers who have had a delay of 1 hour or more.

This way we do two things:

1. Lower the compensation delay time from 3 to 1 hours thus increasing the likelihood of getting paid up to 20 times.

2. We increase the reasons of getting paid from "Airline fault" to ANY (weather for example).

3. You receive your compensation almost instantly (we have to do a few checks to prevent fraud but it will generally take less than a few hours)

Of course these 3 benefits have a price that comes at the expense of your right to receive the full compensation once the 200 to 1 event happens and you have to go through the administrative hell and then wait a few months to receive something, if anything.

So Colibra saves you time, and makes it more convenient for you to use the EU261 directive for a certain fee taken from every compensation we gather from the airline.

In a way we're upgrading the Flight Compensation directive making it fair (3h->1h), convenient (all you have to do is scan your boarding pass) and instantly claimless (you don't have to claim but you'll receive your money ultra fast).

Behaviour science research says people are more likely to choose the lower but instant payout than the higher but uncertain, time & document heavy payout.
