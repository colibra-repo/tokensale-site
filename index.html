How is CoLibra different

Trustless Claims

The claims handling process is moved away from the insurer to an autonomous claim handlers. This removes the conflict of interest between insurer ad client and its incentives to reject claims for profit.

Transparent

Claims handling is decentralized with independent experts voting on claims via our platform on the blockchain

Fully Digital

Fully automated insurance and claim submission process. Artificial Intelligence driven pricing and fraud detection process.
